Brewing Guide
1

Grind

Medium-fine for filter, fine for espresso. Fresh grind is everything.

2

Heat

92–96°C for light roasts, 88–92°C for darker profiles.

3

Bloom

Pour 2x coffee weight, wait 30s. Watch the CO₂ escape.

4

Pour

Controlled spiral pours. 3:30 total time. Patience rewards.
